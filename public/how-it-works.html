The Scoring System

6 dimensions. Real signal.

Our AI doesn't just look at aesthetics. It evaluates the full breadth of a designer's professional competence across six evidence-based dimensions.

01

Visual Craft

Typography, color theory, spatial relationships, grid systems, and overall visual hierarchy quality.

02

Problem Solving

Whether the design demonstrably solves a real user or business problem with clarity and intentionality.

03

Storytelling

Case study depth, narrative clarity, and the ability to communicate design rationale and process.

04

Technical Execution

Prototyping fidelity, component structure, design tooling proficiency, and handoff readiness.

05

Market Fit

Commercial relevance, alignment with real-world design standards, and ability to meet business objectives.

06

Communication

Clarity of written rationale, design documentation quality, and ability to articulate decisions.

FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free for designers?

Yes — creating a profile, getting your AI portfolio review, and completing growth challenges are all free. You only pay if you want premium features in the future.

How do I submit my portfolio?

Share a URL (Behance, Dribbble, personal website, or Notion case study). Our AI will analyze the work within your review session.

How do clients view my score?

Your AI score and dimension breakdown appear on your public Talent Thread profile. Clients can see exactly what you're strong in before reaching out.

What if I'm unhappy with my score?

Your score improves as you complete challenges and re-submit work. The Growth Lab is specifically designed to help you raise weak dimensions over time.

How does client posting work?

Clients post a brief and receive matched applications from AI-verified designers. There's no subscription — you only pay a small fee when you make a hire.

Is the marketplace India-only?

Currently focused on India-based designers and companies, with remote-friendly gigs open to designers from any city.
